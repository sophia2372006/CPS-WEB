Privacy & Policy

At CampNet, your privacy is very important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, place an order, or contact customer support. This includes your name, email address, billing and shipping addresses, and payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to process your orders, provide customer service, improve our services, and communicate with you about promotions or updates.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information with trusted third parties that help us operate our business, such as payment processors and shipping companies, but only as necessary to fulfill your orders.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience and collect analytics data. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Your Choices

You can update or delete your account information by contacting us. You may also opt out of marketing emails at any time.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. We will notify you of any significant changes on our website.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at contact@velvetlane.com.
